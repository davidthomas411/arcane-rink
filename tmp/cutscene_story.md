# Arcane Rink

## Season I: Trials of the Puckbound

---

# CUTSCENE STRUCTURE GUIDE

Cutscenes should:

- Last 5–8 seconds max
- Use 1–3 panels
- Minimal text
- Strong mood
- Fast transition back to gameplay

Tone:
Graphic Arcane Fantasy.
Dark arena atmosphere.
Subtle motion.
Bold impact typography.

---

# Opening Cutscene (First Launch Only)

## Trigger

First time profile enters the Arena.

---

## Panel 1

Visual:
Dark rink surface.
Ice cracks faintly glowing with arcane runes beneath.
Low fog drifting.

Text:
"The ice remembers."

Duration:
2 seconds

---

## Panel 2

Visual:
A rune circle ignites across the arena floor.
Energy pulses outward.

Text:
"Only the Puckbound command its power."

Duration:
2–3 seconds

---

## Panel 3

Visual:
Player portrait fades in.
Subtle rim light.
Runes rotating faintly behind them.

Text:
"Your trial begins."

Transition:
Fade to arena.
HUD activates.

---

# Boss Rune Cutscene

## Trigger

High combo threshold or milestone run.

---

## Panel 1

Visual:
Arena darkens.
All minor runes flicker out.

Text:
"A Greater Gate awakens."

Duration:
2 seconds

---

## Panel 2

Visual:
Massive rune sigil forms mid-air.
Energy fractures outward.

Text:
"Hold your focus."

Transition:
Boss Rune spawns immediately in gameplay.

# Tone Rules

- Keep dialogue short.
- Avoid lore dumps.
- No exposition paragraphs.
- No long cinematic pauses.
- Every cutscene should feel like momentum.

Fantasy world.
Graphic impact.
Return to play quickly.
